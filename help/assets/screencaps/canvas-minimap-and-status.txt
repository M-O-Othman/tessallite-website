Screenshot detail of the Canvas bottom-right quadrant showing the minimap and status bar. The minimap is a 280x180 px rounded panel anchored bottom-right; it renders the whole star-schema in reduced form with node rectangles coloured by type (fact blue, dimension green, pocket violet) and a yellow rectangle showing the current viewport. Clicking-and-dragging inside the minimap pans the canvas. Below the minimap, stretching across the full canvas width, a slim dark status bar renders six badges separated by vertical dividers: "5 tables", "3 joins", "2 aggregates (1 stale)", "1 pocket", "health: green", "last saved 2 min ago". The stale-aggregate badge has a subtle amber dot to indicate caution. A secondary row of micro-icons on the right side of the status bar offers shortcuts for zoom-to-fit, zoom-100%, and toggle-minimap. Dimensions: 1440x360px. Purpose: show how the navigational overview and health-at-a-glance bar cohabit without cluttering the canvas.
